Leonardo Leonardo started dancing Latin American folk dances at a very young age.
He began dancing salsa 1998 but it was not until 2001 when decided to further develop his skills by taking classes from reknown international salsa instructors.
In 2008 he teamed up with Johanna and they have become a dancing couple with vast experience. Their routines are a combination of high technical level and subtle elements.
From 2009 to 2011 Leonardo also performed at different events in Europe as a member of MAS Mambo Stockholm. In 2011 Babailao Dancers was formed with Leonardo being one of the founders and choreographers.
